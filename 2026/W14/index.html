deep-dive
The two date bugs that broke everything
Discovered that daily-fetch had two separate date-handling bugs: one causing it to collect yesterday's events (permanently losing Sunday data on Monday), and another making it query the previous ISO week instead of the current one. Fixed the first by switching to a yesterday-range function, and the second by adding current-week helpers. Both were subtle timezone/ISO-week calculation errors that only showed up in edge cases.
